How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner, fill in your email, choose a secure password, and follow the verification steps.

How do I watch live streaming events?

Live streams are accessible through the Live tab in the menu once you have an active subscription. Make sure your internet speed is stable.

What devices are supported?

CINELIGHT supports streaming on smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, laptops, and desktops via browsers or our dedicated apps.

How do I download a movie?

Open the movie's page, click Download, choose a quality (SD, HD, or 4K), and it will be saved in your CINELIGHT library for offline viewing. Premium required.

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.
