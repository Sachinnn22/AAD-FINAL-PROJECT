Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the Sign Up button at the top right, enter your email and password, then follow the on-screen instructions.

How do I buy premium?

Purchase premium by visiting the Pricing page and choosing the plan that fits you best.

How can I watch live videos?

You can watch live videos by navigating to the Live section once logged in. Make sure you have an active subscription.

How many days can I have access to the free trial?

The free trial gives you access for 7 days to explore all premium features.
